about alice

Hello! My name is Alice.

I am currently a second-year at the University of Toronto, studying Cognitive Science and Visual Studies. While I've always been engaging with the arts, I have grown into someone who deeply enjoys the thinking and technicality digital systems and software requires.

In my free time, I like to bake, swim, and play games. In fact, each year, I set New Years resolutions on what I'd like to achieve in my games, and I actually set out to finish them.
